Bahari Island Resort is located south of Mombasa Island

A boutique adults only property ideal for honeymooners, destination weddings, and couples seeking a romantic getaway.

This unique horseshoe-shaped island paradise boasts virgin tropical rainforest and white sand beaches surrounded by pristine coral reefs. Featuring 12 traditional African designed bures, each with air-conditioning, outdoor stone shower, large sitting area, and all the amenities of a luxury resort.

A boutique adults only resort offering snorkeling, scuba diving, sailing, fishing, kayaking, hiking, private picnics, cultural tours and excursions, fine dining and spa services. Meal Inclusive and Meal Exclusive rates available.
